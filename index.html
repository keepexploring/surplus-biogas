Digester efficiency

—

Production = dung × 0.04 m³/kg × efficiency factor. Efficiency accounts for hydraulic retention time: underloaded = thinner microbial community; overloaded = slurry exits before full digestion. Stove rates: 0.3 m³/hr single, 0.6 m³/hr double (SNV/BSP field standard).

🏗️ Digester reference — loading vs capacity

Optimal loading derived from 30–40 day hydraulic retention time for mesophilic fixed-dome digesters in East Africa (Gurung 1997; SNV/BSP). Below ~25 days HRT, incomplete digestion reduces both gas yield and pathogen kill in the bio-slurry.
